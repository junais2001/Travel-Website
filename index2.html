Grace Bay Beach – Turks and Caicos

Grace Bay Beach is often ranked as one of the most beautiful beaches in the world. It stretches for about 7 miles (11 km) along the northern coast of Providenciales, the most developed island in the Turks and Caicos archipelago.

Whitehaven Beach – Australia

Whitehaven Beach is located on Whitsunday Island, the largest of the Whitsunday Islands off the coast of Queensland, Australia. It is part of the Great Barrier Reef World Heritage Area and is famous for its pure white silica sand, which is 98% silica, giving it a brilliant, almost glowing appearance.

Temples

Angkor Wat – Cambodia

Angkor Wat is the largest religious monument in the world, originally built in the early 12th century as a Hindu temple dedicated to the god Vishnu, but later transformed into a Buddhist temple.

Kashi Vishwanath Temple – India

Kashi Vishwanath Temple, located on the western bank of the Ganges River in the holy city of Varanasi, is one of the most sacred temples in Hinduism. It is dedicated to Lord Shiva, who is worshiped here in his form as Vishwanath, or "Ruler of the Universe."

Country

New Zealand

New Zealand is world-renowned for its stunning and diverse natural landscapes, ranging from rolling green hills to dramatic fjords, pristine beaches, active volcanoes, and lush rainforests.

Switzerland

Switzerland is famed for its stunning Alpine landscapes, crystal-clear lakes, lush green valleys, and charming villages. The country is a year-round destination, offering visitors majestic mountains, serene countryside, and cosmopolitan cities amidst a backdrop of jaw-dropping natural beauty.
